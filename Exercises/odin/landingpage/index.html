Mercedes-AMG GT 4-door Coupé

The Mercedes-AMG GT 63 S E Performance 4-Door Coupé fuses seemingly incompatible vehicle classes into an extravagant package - and that includes design. As the first performance hybrid from Mercedes-AMG, the GT 63 S E PERFORMANCE focuses on stylish accents that clearly identify it as an E PERFORMANCE vehicle.

The combination of performance, design and efficiency is the unique design package for the Mercedes-AMG GT 63 S E PERFORMANCE 4-Door Coupé.

Unmistakable - The extravagant design.

Developed in the wind tunnel, the package improves handling characteristics at high speeds and lays the foundation for even more driving pleasure. The detachable parts increase the contact pressure and reduce the air resistance.

Emphasise the dynamism of your vehicle with a high-tech material from the world of motorsport: detachable parts in carbon-fibre or carbon design visually enhance the exterior from every angle and underline the performance potential of your Mercedes-AMG.

Striking carbon-fibre elements for the exterior underscore the performance credentials of your Mercedes-AMG. The fixed rear aerofoil in carbon-fibre and the outside mirror housings in carbon-fibre design are visible from afar and lend your vehicle an even sportier appearance from any angle.

With the AMG Chrome Package, your vehicle looks particularly elegant and classy. Additional elements in high-gloss chrome underscore the elegance and create contrasting or complementary highlights depending on the vehicle colour.
